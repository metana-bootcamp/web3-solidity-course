# ZK Prerequisites Workshop Series

A series of 6 one-hour workshops covering the foundational mathematics and concepts needed before diving into Zero Knowledge Proofs.

## Workshop Schedule

| # | Topic | Duration |
|---|-------|----------|
| 1 | [Introduction to ZK & Computational Complexity](./01-intro-zk-and-computational-complexity/index.html) | 1 hour |
| 2 | [Finite Fields & Modular Arithmetic](./02-finite-fields-and-modular-arithmetic/index.html) | 1 hour |
| 3 | [Set Theory & Abstract Algebra](./03-set-theory-and-abstract-algebra/index.html) | 1 hour |
| 4 | [Group Theory](./04-group-theory/index.html) | 1 hour |
| 5 | [Homomorphisms](./05-homomorphisms/index.html) | 1 hour |
| 6 | [Elliptic Curve Cryptography](./06-elliptic-curve-cryptography/index.html) | 1 hour |

## Prerequisites for These Workshops

- Familiarity with basic programming (any language)
- Understanding of time complexity and Big O notation
- Basic comfort with mathematical notation
- No prior cryptography experience required

## What Comes After

After completing these prerequisite workshops, students will be ready to tackle:
- ZK-SNARKs (Groth16)
- Circom circuit development
- Bulletproofs
- Number Theoretic Transforms
